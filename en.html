Frequently Asked Questions

More information about integration?

On the DUO Integration website, you can find extensive information about the regulations and exams. You can also find some example exams there to practice with.

Can I get help preparing for the integration exams?

In our open enrollment courses, exam training is included in the lessons. We can also help you with questions about the rules surrounding integration.

How do I know which course is suitable for me?

If you want to register for a course, we would like to make an appointment with you for a free and non-binding intake interview. You can then take a test to determine your language level as accurately as possible.

Can I learn Dutch from my own language?

Even someone who only speaks Chinese, Spanish, or Polish can learn Dutch without it being from their own language. Translations are available for every textbook in sometimes up to 20 different languages. It is our job to deal with this and after the first lesson, everyone speaks a few words of Dutch.

Where are the lessons given?

The group courses with open enrollment are given in Brielle.

The courses for employees can be provided at your company or at a location chosen in consultation.
